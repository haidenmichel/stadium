Our Story

Built on the Field.
Proven at Scale.

StadiumShine Pro was founded by Sterling Michel — a lifelong sports fan and serial entrepreneur who brings a rare blend of technology discipline and genuine love for the game to the world of commercial stadium maintenance.

Sterling's background is in software and technology consulting. Before launching StadiumShine Pro, he built and exited a healthcare technology company and has operated a technology consulting firm for over a decade. He applied that same systems-thinking approach to stadium operations — treating every venue as a complex infrastructure problem that demands precision, scheduling, and scale.

A former semi-pro soccer player out of New York and an avid collector of sports memorabilia (including a signed Denver Nuggets 2023 championship jersey), Sterling built StadiumShine Pro because he understood what these venues mean to the fans who fill them — and how much the environment matters to that experience.
